Code Quest



Brief description: Code Quest is a top-down adventure RPG with programming-related puzzles and themes. The player advances through levels, beating bosses and improving user stats. Levels get progressively more difDicult as the story progresses. There are four levels.

Design process: In the first days of production my partner and I worked on our project plan for the game, hashing out art themes, game mechanics, and general details. Secondarily, we worked primarily on game tutorials and cutscenes, allowing us to get familiar with the game we were making. Once the tutorials were completed, we spent time working on more complex elements such as inventory systems, saving player info, and working on the initial design of combat. The most challenging element in the design process was combat programming. At Dirst we were unable to achieve our desired method, but after some consultation with our instructor, we were able to develop a way of getting what we wanted. The last half of our production primarily involved making new levels based on the existing templates we had already created, removing bugs, and user testing.
