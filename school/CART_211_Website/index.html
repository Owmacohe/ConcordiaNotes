Welcome. Let's begin.


  • The website that you are about to visit is an interactive library of sorts that uses your collaboration to help expand upon and explore the various subcategories of a given topic.
  • Starting with the broad and ambiguous aspects of life, it's up to you to select, browse, and add to their various "subcategories".
  • Click to select a category, and it will split into some of its possible aspects. Select one of these, and the process continues.
  • The first few categories are already populated, but you'll soon find that it's primarily up to you to continue the process.
  • Your added categories are saved while you stay on the page, but are lost as soon as you leave, so be careful!
  • As you browse, you will be able to access a rudimentary map with which you will be able to return to previously viewed categories.
  • For more information about this project, please visit the proposal.
