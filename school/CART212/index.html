Project Brief

Tagline:

A short-form web game addressing the fleeting 'normalacy' that we humans seem to try to cling to during various moments of our lives.

Gameplay description:

Over the course of 2 minutes, the player will have a limited sort of control over a simulated human as the grow old, tend to their bodily needs, and make life decisions. The game's speed, complexity and format will change rapidly as the player progresses, simulating the turbulent and inconsistend changes one goes through in life. As the game changes, it will be blantantly and overtly informing the player to 'adapt', and that this most recent change is 'the new normal'.

Software used:

- HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (programming)
- Aseprite (pixel art)
- Premiere Pro / After Effects (visual effects and/or cutscenes)
