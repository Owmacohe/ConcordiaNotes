Manifesto for creativity

Jump to journal here

Demand #1:

All artistic creativity should and must be recognized for its importance in society.

Demand #2:

All created work should and must containt within it an aspect of artistic creativity.

Demand #3:

All created work that does not contain within it an aspect of artistic creativity should and must be recognized as not fulfilling its potential importance in society.

Demand #4:

All members of society should and must strive to uphold the first 3 demands.
