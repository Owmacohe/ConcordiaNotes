Rules

• To hit with a weapon, roll 1d20 >= of target's ARMR
• To hit with a spell, roll 1d20 <= caster's MAGI (if target is unwilling)
• Spells cost 2 MAGI to cast
• When HLTH = 0, roll 1d20 >= 10 each turn to stay alive
• Advantage / Disadvantage = Roll twice and take highest / lowest
• To move in stealth, roll 1d20 <= character's DEXT
• Other checks roll 1d20 <= respective stat (STRN/DEXT/INTL/MAGI)

• Sessions should last from ~30-60 minutes
• Sessions should contain 1-2 simple combat sequences
• Use these same rules to create NPCs or enemies
• Tweak generated NPCs or enemies to be more or less powerful
