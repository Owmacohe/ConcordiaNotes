Background text will be comprised of very low to high opacity text, to give the feel of ASCII art.

Background text will light up in certain areas as player interacts with the game. Text may even alter to display certain messages.

Simple pixel art for pickup items, companions, etc. Compliments the background text.

Buttons and clickable items will be thick and analog-reminiscent.

Colour Scheme

[=]

Bright green

Used for nearly all text and background elements.

[!]

Transparent green

Backgrounds for most text blocks and buttons. Background text fades in and out of opacity.

[+]

Bright pink

Complementary to main colour. Used for very important details and stand-out words.

[0]

Transparent pink

Backgrounds for pink text blocks and buttons.

Logo Design

Main game logo. Will appear on title screen, loading screens, etc.

Simplified skull can be used for Internet profiles and smaller representations of the logo.
